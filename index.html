Our Aims/ Objectives

  • To take care of the education of less privilege children

  • To visit the homes of the less privilege with Educational materials.

  • To give scholarship to outstanding children.

  • To ameliorate & checkmate the excessibeness of the youth in cultism.

  • To checkmate hooliganism & vandalism amongs the youth.

  • To open an arientation centres to the less privilege and destitute.

  • To encorage vocational training for the less fortunate youths.

  • To open children's home and rescue centres for orphans and vulnarable children.
